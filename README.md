# Sales & Finance Analytics Project

## Project Overview

1. This project focuses on building interactive Sales and Finance Reports using Excel, Power Query, Power Pivot, and DAX to analyze business performance and support data-driven decision-making.

2. The reports help organizations monitor sales trends, evaluate financial performance, track KPIs, and identify business growth opportunities.

---

# Sales Report

## Project Objectives

1. Create a Customer Performance Report

2. Conduct a comprehensive comparison between Market Performance and Sales Targets

## Purpose of Sales Analytics

- Monitor and evaluate sales performance
- Identify sales patterns and trends
- Track important Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Support strategic business decisions

## Key Insights from Reports

The reports assist in:

1. Identifying effective customer discount strategies

2. Supporting customer negotiations

3. Discovering high-potential markets for business expansion

4. Improving overall sales performance



# Finance Report

## Project Objectives

1. Create Profit & Loss (P&L) Reports by Fiscal Year

2. Create Profit & Loss (P&L) Reports by Markets

---

## Purpose of Finance Analytics

Finance analytics helps organizations:

- Evaluate financial performance
- Support business decision-making
- Improve communication with stakeholders
- Build a strong foundation for budgeting and forecasting

---

## Importance of Financial Analysis

- Benchmark performance against previous periods and industry standards
- Align financial planning with business goals
- Build confidence in the organization’s financial outlook
